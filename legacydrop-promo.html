Send and Recieve Photos in a Snap!

Picture this: You want to send something to your friend, and fast. Just use LegacyDrop! You just find and click your friend's iPhone/iPad/iPod touch*, choose the picture, and done!

Send Files to Anyone on your network

Even if you're sharing with someone in another room, as long as they're within Bluetooth or Wi-Fi proximity**, you can share with them.



*: No other devices are supported at this time.
**: Success may vary depending on proximity to the network/device.

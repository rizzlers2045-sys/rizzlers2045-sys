LegacyDrop

Send and Receive Photos

Just select the person you want to share with's iPhone, iPad, ior Pod touch*, choose your file, and you're done!

Compatibility

Share files with any iOS 4+ iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch within Bluetooth or Wi-Fi proximity**.



*: No other devices are supported at this time.
**: Success may vary depending on connection strength with the device and Wi-Fi network.
